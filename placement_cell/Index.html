About us

Government Polytechnic College is Located in Kothamangalam, ‘the gateway of High Range’. The college islocated in a rural area in Ernakulam district with tribal settlement around it. However it is just 7 km away from National Highway 49. The institution is 60 Km away from Ernakulam town and only 40 Km from Kochi International Airport. The nearest railway station is Aluva. The proximity to many famous tourist spot makes its location a special one. The polytechnic is 8 Km away from the famous Thattekkad bird sanctuary, 7 km from Bhoothathankettu, the site of Periyar Valley Irrigation Project and 20 km from Pooyamkutty, the Hydro-Electric Power generation site.

Students get guidance and service of the placement cell to know about better employment opportunities and to get placement according to their interests. Ninety percentage of students who pass out from the college-who don’t go for higher studies –are got placed soon after their course.
