Brief History of Government Polytechnic College, Kothamangalam

Government Polytechnic College has been imparting technical education to students for the last three decades. It had a humble beginning in 1983 with a small intake of 60 students (20 per branch) in three branches of engineering discipline-Mechanical, Civil and Electronics.
At present the college is a known name among the 43 Government Polytechnics in the state of Kerala. It functions under the department of technical education. It offers six semester diploma programme in the four branches of engineering discipline-Mechanical, Civil, Electronics and Computer. The college has a total intake of 224 students every year.
